Scott Road are a Canadian rock band from Surrey, BC formed in 2024,1 currently consisting of Rishon C. (guitar, vocals), Stephanie L. (synths, vocals, guitar), Rapha P. (bass), Dylan T. (drums, guitar), and Fernando V. (vocals, guitar, bass, synths)

The Music

We're currently still working on our sound. If you want genres, we're best described as alternative rock post-hardcore, punk, and shoegaze.

I can't speak for the other members, but I'm mostly pulling from black midi; Black Country, New Road; Squid, Kinoko Teikoku, Moss Icon, foxtails, Asian Glow, Weatherday, and probably a lot more.


The Site

The site's mimics Wikipedia's design, with some creative liberties. I tried my best to borrow elements of its iconic design, like the table, the layout, the small "From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia" tagline, etc.

At that point, it was my biggest/most ambitious site design from scratch. I didn't use any code from Wikipedia. It was all just reverse engineering the look. (Okay, I did look at the source a bit, but I didn't copy/paste anything. I think.)
